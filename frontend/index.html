◆ About MUST Health Tech (MHT)

×

MUST Health Tech (MHT) is a comprehensive Electronic Health Record (EHR) and Hospital Management System designed for the Malawi University of Science and Technology (MUST) Teaching Hospital.

Modules: Patient Registry, Appointments & Queue, Clinical Documentation, Vital Signs, Inpatient Ward, Critical Care, Dialysis & CKD, Laboratory, Pharmacy, Inventory, Billing, Governance, Admin

Demo Credentials:

Staff:ehealth_mw / must@ghs
Patient:MHT-000001 / 0999123456

Key Features: Real-time dashboard, offline support with IndexedDB caching, responsive design for mobile/desktop, patient portal with bill payment, WhatsApp notifications, disease surveillance, and medical education tracking.

◆ Smart Health, Smarter Malawi ◆

Universal Result Interpreter

Paste lab results text, type manually, or upload a scan/image. The AI parses and interprets results.

OCR active (Tesseract + EasyOCR + PaddleOCR fallbacks). Upload a clear lab report scan for automatic extraction.

Test Procedure Guide

Enter a test name to view the full procedure — pre-analytical through post-analytical — with APA 7th edition references.
